This project analyzes real-world Shopify eCommerce sales data from a swimwear brand using R and SQL. The goal is to generate business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends.

Objectives
Analyze seasonal sales trends
Identify best-selling swimwear products
Compare returning vs new customers
Explore customer retention patterns
Evaluate conversion and cart abandonment behavior
Support business decision-making through data visualization
Technologies & Tools
R
SQL
Shopify CSV exports
dplyr
ggplot2
tidyverse
Planned Analysis
1. Best Selling Bikinis by Season

Analyze which products perform best during different seasons and identify seasonal demand patterns.

2. Revenue Trends

Explore monthly and seasonal revenue growth over time.

3. Returning vs New Customers

Compare purchasing behavior between first-time and returning customers.

4. Customer Retention

Measure repeat purchase behavior and customer retention rates.

5. Conversion Funnel

Analyze customer journey stages from product views to completed purchases.

6. Cart Abandonment

Identify abandoned cart patterns and potential revenue loss.

7. Revenue Forecasting

Build forecasting models to estimate future sales trends.

Business Value

The analysis aims to support:

inventory planning
product strategy
marketing optimization
customer retention initiatives
revenue forecasting
